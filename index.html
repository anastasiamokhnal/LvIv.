Welcome to Lviv

First of all this is not about touristic places that you can easily find in the net (but they are also amazing). It is about atmosphere that you must feel because Lviv isn't run-of-the-mill place. One of the things that everyone likes is street musicians. You won't be able steer clear them and you shouldn't. Just dance or sing along with them

Old city with it's own beauty. Buildings and streets that will caught your eye. there is a street with several names: as of 2013, Archivnaya Street was named after Bergman, Fellini, Truffaut, Ilyenko, Parajanov and Tarkovsky. Get unforgettable experience. You won't regret

Walking up and down the streets you can only imagine how much events those walls have seen. Everything around you is breathing history. It is alive past that you can touch. By the way, due to the similarity with the capital of France, it was in Lviv that M. Boyarsky's film "The Three Musketeers" was once shot.

Some interest facts about Lviv:

❤The city's slogan is "Lviv is open to the world".

❤Lviv has the status of the official cultural capital of Ukraine. It was awarded in 2009.

❤If you want to see all beauty of Lviv you should watch official music video "То є Львів" filmed by famous and talented musician group "Скрябін".

❤(And one more interesting fact to make you laugh a little bit) :

A significant part of the city's streets is covered with paving stones. In the chronicles there is a record of such interesting facts about Lviv: in 1780, the Emperor of Austria was driving around Lviv and got stuck in a carriage in a pile of garbage. It took several good oxen to pull out the emperor's transport. After this incident, the emperor ordered to completely cover the square with paving stones.

Maybe here you wont got as brown as a berry but you can be sure that round-the-clock you will be amazed. This place wont get you bored and you fast get into the swing of it♥
